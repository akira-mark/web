Shipping Information

Learn more about our shipping options, delivery times, and fees.

Shipping Options

  • Standard Shipping: Delivered within 5-7 business days.
  • Express Shipping: Delivered within 2-3 business days.
  • Overnight Shipping: Delivered the next business day.

Shipping Fees

  • Standard Shipping: $5.99 (Free for orders over $50).
  • Express Shipping: $15.99.
  • Overnight Shipping: $25.99.

International Shipping

We offer international shipping to select countries. Delivery times and fees vary depending on the destination. Please contact our customer service team at support@stridelux.com for more details.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. Use this number to track your order on our website or the carrier's website.
